Stash

Looking for an article you saw before? SearchStash lets you select text on a webpage and stash it for future reference.

Its private, just visible to you, and accessible from any computer.

Simply highlight the text you'd like to store, right click, and select Stash It!

Search

When time comes to find an article you've seen before, SearchStash has your back.

To search for an article, click Search Stash from the plugin and search for a keyword.
