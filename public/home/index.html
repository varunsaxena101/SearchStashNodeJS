Stash

Looking for an article you saw before? SearchStash lets you select text on a webpage and stash it for future reference.

It's private, just visible to you, and accessible from any computer.

Simply highlight the text you'd like to save, right click, and select Stash It!

Search

When time comes to find an article you've seen before, SearchStash has your back.

To search for an article, click Search Stash on the browser plugin and type keywords into the text box.
